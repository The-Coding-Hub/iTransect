About iTransect

About Web App

This application is an AI and NLP enabled language detector cum translator. It can first of all detect the language used in the text entered by the user. Then it can also convert the text in your desired language. This app has a capability to recognize and translate text to over 15 languages.

App Requirement

We frequently face problems while reading google articles or while going through websites which are not in english language or our mother tongue. Many rural people also don't understand any language except their Mother Tongue. So they can also translate the text and go through it.

My Solution

My Solution for the above stated problem is that we can create a translator to translate the text into a language which we can understand. But the problem is that we need to first recognize that the original text is written in which language and mostly we fail to do so. For this reason my application would just take the text as input, recognize the language of the text and then it would also translate the text into our desired language.

App Working

In this app I have used the concepts of the “Natural Language Processing” Domain of “Artificial Intelligence”. We have used the Bag of Words Algorithm and Concepts of Text PreProcessing. I have also used a huge dataset of more than 10,000 rows. This dataset contains over 15 languages and texts on every language. I have also used Models of the Scikit-Learn Library to predict the language used in a text.
